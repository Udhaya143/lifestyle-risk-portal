About LifeStyle Check

LifeStyle Check is a comprehensive risk assessment platform designed to evaluate your susceptibility to lifestyle-related diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, hypertension, and obesity.

Our evidence-based algorithm analyzes your lifestyle choices, family history, and health metrics to provide personalized risk scores and actionable recommendations to improve your health outcomes.
